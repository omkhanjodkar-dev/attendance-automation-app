# WaveLog User Guide

Welcome to **WaveLog**, your smart attendance automation system! This guide will help students and faculty members get started with the mobile app and web dashboard.

## For Students: The Mobile App

**Goal**: Mark your attendance in seconds using your phone.

### Step 1: Download the App
Get the app from the Google Play Store:
**[Download WaveLog for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.example.wavelog_dummy)** *(Link is a placeholder)*

### Step 2: Login
1.  Open the app.
2.  Enter your **Student Email-ID** (e.g., `name.surname25@pccoepune.org`) and **Password (e.g., your PRN Number)**.
3.  Tap **Login**.

### Step 3: Mark Attendance
1.  **Be in Class**: Ensure you are physically present in the classroom.
2.  On the home screen, you will see your current location status.
3.  If a class is active, tap the big **"Mark Attendance"** button.
4.  **Success!** You will see a confirmation message.

## For Faculty: The Dashboard & App

**Goal**: Manage class sessions and view attendance reports.

### The Faculty Web Dashboard
Use this for detailed analytics and history.

**Access Link**: **[Faculty Dashboard](https://attendance-automation-app-faculty.onrender.com)**

#### **1. Logging In**
- Visit the dashboard link above.
- Enter your **Faculty Email-ID** and **Password**.
- Click **Access Dashboard**.

#### **2. Overview Tab**
- **Real-time Stats**: See how many students are present right now.
- **Charts**: View a "Pass vs. Fail" attendance pie chart.
- **Subject Filter**: Use the dropdown (e.g., "CPPS") to see stats for a specific subject.

#### **3. Attendance History Tab**
- Click the **"Attendance History"** tab to see a detailed log of every student's attendance.
- The list is sorted by time, so the most recent check-ins appear at the top.

### The Faculty Mobile App
Use this to **start** a class session.

1.  Login to the WaveLog app with your Faculty credentials.
2.  Click the **"Start Session"** button for your subject.
3.  Tap **"Start Attendance Session"**.
4.  Students can now mark their attendance!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why do I have to login again when I open a new tab?**
A: For security reasons, your session is private to each tab. If you close a tab, you are logged out automatically to protect student data.
